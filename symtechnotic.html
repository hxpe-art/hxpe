symtechnotic, 2023

Symtechnotic presents a post-patriarchal future reimagining structures of time, biology, and community. Through speculative installation and material exploration, this work blurs the boundaries between the digital and the natural, human and non-human. Kinetic structure changing in accordance with sourced average menstrual data. This structure uses data from estrogen/progesterone levels and follicular development throughout the average regular cycle to control light, color, and motion. It is scaled to 30 seconds=30 days, serving as an abstracted visualization of the menstrual cycle.
