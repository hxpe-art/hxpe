Hope Barkov is a Chicago based artist, technologist, curator, educator, and cyberfeminist. Her work explores solidarity, biology, femininity, resistance, symbiosis, and human connection in the digital age. Hope has a BFA from the School of The Art Institute of Chicago with an emphasis in print media and art and technology studies. She currently works as in-house curator at ]SPACE.01] gallery and teaches robotics. She is passionate about art, science, and tech accessibillity, cowboy boots, and dogs.
